Project Funders

Delivery Phase

Paul Hamlyn Foundation

Gaslight Media Trust (GMT) would like to thank Paul Hamlyn Foundation for supporting the JustUs project’s delivery phase and more specifically, supporting GMT in ‘Facilitating the Strategic Rollout of the JustUs Workshop Education Programme’.

Development Phase

Special EU Programme's Body

Gaslight Media Trust were fortunate in being able to secure all the public funding for this project from a single source, the EU Peace III Programme, managed by the Special EU Programme’s Body.  As ever developing such innovative projects is never straght forward and we would like to acknowledge the support of all the management and staff of the SEUPB and the Pobal/Community Relations Council Consortium who have worked with us in overseeing its development.

 This project has been supported by the EU Peace III Programme, managed by the Special EU Programme’s Body.
