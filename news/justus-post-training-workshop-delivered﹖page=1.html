JustUs Post Training Workshop Delivered

GMT hosted a post training workshop for participants of its first ‘Facilitating JustUs Training the Trainer Programme’ in early February.

PEACE IV DCSDC Spring 18 Newsletter.pdf

The workshop took place in Foyle Arena, Derry city and afforded trainees / participants an opportunity to reflect and discuss participant learning post our two-day training programme, review and trouble shoot any issues that have arisen for participants with regard to their use of JustUs within their respective organisations.

We are happy to report that of the original 16 youth education practitioners who participated in our training programme back in October, 11 of those are now successfully using the JustUs resource with their respective groups of young people.

During the workshop, project participants expressed great enthusiasm for the programme and several of them also related individual stories about how some young people they are working with have been positively affected by the JustUs educational approach.
