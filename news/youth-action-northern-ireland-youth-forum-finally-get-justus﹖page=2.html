Youth Action & Northern Ireland Youth Forum Finally Get JustUs

Senior and experienced youth workers from two of the north's leading youth education providers, namely Youth Action and the Northern Ireland Youth Forum participated in GMT’s ‘Facilitating JustUs Training The Trainer Programme’ in late September 2018.

GMT's JustUs training the trainer programme was hosted by Youth Action (YA) in its Belfast offices over two consecutive days in late September 2018 and it seen 10 youth workers from YA join with 5 youth worker colleagues from the Northern Ireland Youth Forum (NIYF) in participating in the programme's series of workshops.

Having participated in two days of 'Facilitating JustUs Training' senior youth workers with Youth Action, Emma Johnston and Maria Perkins give their views on that experience and on the JustUs Workshop Education Programme itself.
